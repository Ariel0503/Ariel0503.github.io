# about

About me

Hi, I am Martina from Paris, France. I have over 15 years of experience combining software engineering and strategy consulting. 
My job is to help businesses find solutions to move their company forward sustainably. Moreover, My work is completely based on expertise and data. 
I worked for big international firms and scaleups.

- Global leadership & Business acumen - Upto 50 member team, Locations: US, India, Germany and France. 
- Business proposals, recruitment, procurement, financials, target setting and performance management & KPIs.
- External/internal client relationship - Team lead to C-levels.
- Software engineering - End to end software development using agile methodologies and design thinking.
