About me

I am a Business management consultant with an MBA from prestigious european business school and 10 years of software engineering background. I possess skills for data analysis, client relations and international team management.

- Global leadership & Business acumen : Upto 50 member team for fortune 500 companies, Locations: US, India, Germany and France. 
- Business skills : Varied consulting engagements in automotive & food industry, with an MBA from reknown HEC Paris Business School.
- Market research & Data analysis :> Certified expert in Power BI. Quantitative and qualitative analysis based on study of public & private databases, competitor research, product evaluations, financial reports, expert interviews and surveys. 
- Software engineering : Proven expertise in end to end software development using agile methodologies and design thinking in automotive and health care.
